Background

Koon Ming Shoe Trading Sdn Bhd is a company that is specialized in manufacturing and importing a variety of types of shoes from a PU casual footwear that is suitable for uniform groups and office use, heavy duty safety shoes that protect the wearer foot, and even the sandals that are comfortable to wear on the local humid weather. We have over 20 years experiences in supplying a wide variety of footwear that is suitable for different levels of needs from the vast amount of customers. Thus, the experiences we had and the reliable services we provided had helped in building a good reputation in foot wear manufacturing industry.



Mission & Vision

The mission of Koon Ming Shoe Trading Sdn. Bhd. is to become one of the largest foot wear manufacturer world wide. We are committed to continuously strengthen our brand name and improve our products and services. The director and the founder of Koon Ming Shoe Trading Sdn. Bhd., Mr Lee Wai Cheong said, "Everyone deserved a better walk.". With his passions and knowledge in manufacturing the foot wear products and his expertise in business management, he is confident to bring everyone a better walk with his products.
